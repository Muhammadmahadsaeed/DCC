Waste Water Treatment Plant

One of the biggest danger to marine life is the huge volumes of waste water continuously drained directly into rivers, streams and the ocean itself. The principle objective of industrial waste water treatment plants is to allow liquid waste discharge to be disposed of without damage to human health and the environment.
